Blog post 1:Cakey, Chewy, Fudgy

posted by brownielover on April 29, 2017

Everyone loves brownies but some like them nice dense and rich, while others like a more soft and fluffy texture.

With so many types of brownies, the question is "how does a baker achieved their desired texture?" The trick is to have different proportions of fat (i.e. butter and chocolate), sugar, flour and eggs.

Cakey Chewy Fudgey
Butter cold butter softened butter melted butter or oil
Chocolate powdered cocoa half cococa half melted chocolate melted chocolate
Flour more flour with baking powder same amount of flour
Eggs has same amout of eggs less whole eggs best with more egg yolks
Sugar white sugar brown sugar more white sugar than cakey
